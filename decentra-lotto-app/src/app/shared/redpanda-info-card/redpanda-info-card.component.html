About Red Panda

We are an animal and wildlife welfare charity token on Ethereum (ERC-20)!

We’ve completed over 100 Donations worldwide, have partnerships with charitable organizations (RainForest Trust and International Animal Rescue) and are fully backed by a Doxxed team and a non-profit foundation in USA!

We’ve been around since April 2021 and have over 8,000 holders worldwide and growing. Check us out at www.redpanda.earth and follow our progress at https://twitter.com/redpandatoken! You can also mint one of our Red Panda Pals at nft.redpanda.earth!

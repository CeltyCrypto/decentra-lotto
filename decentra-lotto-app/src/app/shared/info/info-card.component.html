How To

Use the "Ticket Swap" panel above to buy a ticket to enter the current draw using BNB, BUSD, USDT, USDC or DAI. Draws last for one week.

The number of winning tickets depends on the size of the pot - the bigger the pot, the more winners. The minimum you can win is $10 (the price of a ticket). There is no maximum win amount.

Once you buy a ticket, your total tickets, winning chance and other stats can be seen in the "Your Stats" panel.

The Ethereum lottery is extremely simple to save you on GAS - there are no ticket purchases needed and you are automatically entered into lottery draws just by holding the DELO token on Ethereum. There is a minimum hold requirement of 82,000,000 DELO (0.1% of the supply) to be automatically entered into every draw. If you hold less than this, you will not be in with a chance to win the jackpot.

All you have to do to enter is buy and hold at least 82,000,000 DELO ETH, or bridge accross from BSC to ETH. Draws happen automatically once the "Current Pot" has met the threshold "Next Draw At" above. Once this happens, Chainlink is used to fairly draw a winner from the DELO ETH token holders.
